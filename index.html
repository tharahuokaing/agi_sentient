Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)

Focused entirely on cognitive capability. An AGI system can learn, reason, adapt, and perform any intellectual task that a human being can do across diverse domains without specialized retraining.

Sentient Artificial Intelligence

Defined by conscious experience (Qualia). A Sentient AI possesses subjective internal experiences, self-awareness, and the capacity to genuinely feel emotions like joy, sorrow, or pain.

The Key Distinction

Simply put: AGI is about doing; Sentience is about being. An AI could hypothetically be an exceptional, super-intelligent AGI problem solver while remaining completely cold, hollow, and non-sentient.
